SOLO


If you pick Solo, all you have to do is enter your bet and your desired color it's up to you if you want to choose two or more color. After that you will submit your bet and proceed to roll. If you win, your bet will became double or tripple.

MULTIPLAYER


If you pick Multiplayer, this is the game where your friends and family can be join. Basically, in this system it will show only the color using randomizer and you or your friends either family will play with or without bet, it's up to you guys!!
